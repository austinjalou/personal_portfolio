About me

Hello world! My name is Austin Jalou. I was born in 1995, and I grew up and have lived in the Metro Detroit area of Michigan for my entire life. Although I am new to web development, and previously do not have very much experience with it, I feel like I will do exceptionally well. I have a strong work ethic and a passion for learning new skills. I decided to take the online Coding Boot Camp course offered by Michigan State University for my own interest. I did not originally plan on using this course to change my career path, but after getting familiar with a few programming languages and exploring the web development world, I have been considering moving forward with a web development future. On this website you will find a basic guide of some of my work as a beginner in web development, as well as ways to get in contact with me.
